About Sway

My Journey – From Struggles to Success

I’m Josue Isaac Marroquin Sandoval, but most people know me as Sway. My journey into trading and financial markets wasn’t an easy one. Before I found success, I went through some of the most challenging years of my life. From personal struggles to financial uncertainty, I faced obstacles that forced me to rebuild myself from the ground up.

One of the biggest turning points in my life was when I discovered trading. At first, it was overwhelming—I had no mentor, no clear direction, and I made plenty of mistakes. But over time, I obsessed over learning, studying charts, analyzing markets, and understanding risk management. I journaled my wins and losses, tracked my mindset, and kept going no matter what.

I realized that financial freedom is possible, but only for those who are willing to put in the work. Trading became more than just a way to make money—it became a tool for personal growth, discipline, and long-term success.
